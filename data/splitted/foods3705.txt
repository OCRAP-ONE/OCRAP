After googling a bit about making waffles fluffy I found the solution!<br /><br />First separate your egg. Put the whites in a desperate bowl from the yolk. Then use a mixer to beat the whites until 'stiff'. You can do by hand but it does take a few minutes and Pretty rigourous whisking but Itll happen. Basically you want the whites to be so fluffy and frothy that they don't move - kinda like meringue.<br /><br />Now mix your yolks and the water together and mix really well. Then slowly mix the flour in being careful not to over mix. Shouldn't take long.<br /><br />Now the fun part (Google this for a video too if you don't know this term) Fold the egg whites into your batter. The better you do this without over mixing or deflating the whites, the fluffier the waffles will be.<br /><br />Don't know why they don't say that on the box but I did this and just had the best waffles I've ever had! Good luck!
